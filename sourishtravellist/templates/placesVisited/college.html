My College Journey

After completing my schooling in Agartala, I appeared for WBJEE and went through the counselling process. I got admitted to the Birbhum Institute of Engineering and Technology in the Electronics and Communication Engineering branch.

These years laid the groundwork for my higher education and professional journey, instilling in me strong values of hard work, teamwork, and continuous growth.

Hostel life was a mix of chaos and charm—late-night study sessions, shared meals, and endless laughter. It was here that I built friendships that felt more like family, with people who stood by me through every high and low.

College life taught me not just academics, but resilience, teamwork, and self-discovery. My engineering journey was filled with challenges, innovations, and the joy of turning ideas into reality—an experience that shaped both my career and character.
