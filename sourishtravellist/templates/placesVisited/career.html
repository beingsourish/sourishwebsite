My Work Journey

My professional journey began in 2012 when I joined Tata Consultancy Services (TCS) in Kolkata, India, as a Software Engineering Trainee. This role laid the foundation of my career in software development and IT solutions.

In 2015, I moved to Nashville, Tennessee, where I worked as a Software Developer for the California Testing Bureau (CTB). This was my first international experience, broadening my understanding of global work environments and collaborative projects.

The following year, 2016, I transitioned to Monterey, California, joining DRC (Data Recognition Corporation). My work involved educational data systems and assessments, expanding my skills in data-driven development and large-scale applications.

In February 2017, I relocated to Tampa, Florida, to work with Verizon as a Data Engineer. I was part of a major data migration project, contributing to robust ETL pipelines and scalable infrastructure, which enhanced my expertise in data engineering and analytics.

Each city and role in my journey exposed me to new challenges, cultures, and perspectives.
